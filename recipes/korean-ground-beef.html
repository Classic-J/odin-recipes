Korean Ground Beef

Description

Ever craved some Korean food but couldn't stomach the price tag?

Well I have a treat for you! Introducing a beautifully balanced Korean Ground Beef recipe that pairs supremely well with white rice that is sure to be something you can stomach! This budget friendly and super simple recipe will have you in and out in 30 minutes or less.

This recipe features minimal ingredients and low effort and can be enhanced by adding nearly anything else to it as you wish. Think big like vegetables, hot chili paste, or even add this recipe to another. The possibilities are endless!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef (90% lean)
  • 3 garlic cloves minced
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 2 teaspoons sesame oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teapsoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 2 cups hot cooked white or brown rice
  • sliced green onions and sesame seeds for garnish

Steps

  1. In a large skillet cook the ground beef and garlic breaking it into crumbles over medium heat until no longer pink. Drain the grease.
  2. In a small bowl whisk brown sugar, soy sauce, sesame oil, ginger, red pepper flakes and pepper. Pour over the ground beef and let simmer for another minute or two.
  3. Serve over hot rice and garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
